Founded The Sishya Launchx Entrepreneurship Club (2017-18)

MIT Launch Club is a platform offered to motivated high school students. Using online materials and guidance from the club leaders (who are also students), club members undertake a journey during which they form teams, ideate, and create real companies with real products.

I am the Founder, President, and Education Lead of my school's Launch Club, called the Sishya LaunchX Entrepreneurship Club. Started in September 2017, the main focus of the club is to build products and companies related to 'Environmental Sustainability' - the theme for our club. Being the Education Lead means that I'm responsible for teaching the course material to the student members every week. As School President, I attend monthly webinars given by the MIT Launch Coordinators and convey my learnings to my peers in the form of a brief, watered-down presentation.

The club members have come up with extremely innovative ideas to solve problems such as waste management, recycling of waste water, and conservation of energy.
