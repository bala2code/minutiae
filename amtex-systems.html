About DVI's Vantage Point

DVI's Vantage Point is a data visualization engine designed to illustrate the whole program. In this project, I was exposed to understanding how data is collected from multiple sources and presented in a dashboard form created in an intuitive way. The team at Amtex aggregated expense, travel management and credit card data, industry benchmarks and other value added data sources to create a full view of the client company's travel spend and traveller behaviour.

Vantage Point eliminates the need to view multiple reports or manually aggregate disparate data into spreadsheets. Visualizations tell a story much like an infographic. Vantage Point can provide immediate analysis, comparisons and trends. The information presented can be filtered in multiple ways. A huge advantage of this presentation of data is that traditional reports largely become out of date soon after they are created, while visualization can change as new data is incorporated.

Vantage Point's charts, graphs, maps and gauges allow one to look at the program from many perspectives. The client company can see overall travel spend, air spend, hotel spend, rental car spend, traveler analysis, ancillary travel spend and compliance metrics.

Every visualization is live, modular and actionable allowing users to filter on data points exclude data points and group data points for comparison analysis.

For me it was an understanding of how data presentation is personalised to the needs of client travel companies as well as how visual presentation is crucial for ease of understanding and analysis. To that end, I contributed to the design of the user interface.
