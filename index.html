Founded The Sishya Launchx Entrepreneurship Club (2017-18)

The Sishya LaunchX Entrepreneurship Club is a club that I've founded in my school in 2017. MIT, in collaboration with LaunchX, introduced an online platform whereby high school students could join an 'MIT Launch Club' run by students in their respective schools, learn about what it truly means to be an entrepreneur, and turn their ideas for a product or business into an actual company. Student Volunteer for the 'Adarsh Gram Yojana Project' (2016-17)

FICCI FLO Chennai, an organization solely run by women, has adopted four villages - Venkatapuram, Sasthirambakkam, Thellimedu and Mettu Colony - under the Gram Yojana Scheme. I volunteered as a student to help teach Mathematics to the children in the Venkatapuram and Mettu Colony villages, as well as help raise funds and collect soaps and other resources to improve the health and sanitation levels of these villages.
