Analysing data for revenue growth - Hotel Crowne Plaza, Adyar Park

Scope:

To increase revenue by targeting weekend occupancy

Action Plan:

  • Rationalise pricing to increase overall weekend revenue
  • Target wholesale business during weekend

Key reasons impacting weekend occupancy:

  • Absence of airline crew contracts supporting the occupancy all week
  • Low wholesale and leisure targeted B2B agencies
  • Aggressive price competition during weekends over domestic leisure guests

Data used to draw conclusions:

  1. 60-90 Days competition Price review
  2. Understanding the city demand via Demand calendar
  3. Utilising the STR reports to understand the trends
  4. Utilising PERFORM to understand the Demand forward looking

1. 60 - 90 Days Online rate comparison:

Its has been observed that the city hotels have been very aggressive on the online platform both on brand and non-branded hotels. Without impacting the rate parity, we have planned for the rationalizing the online price on a daily basis to be more competitive on the online platform. Rate review was done twice a day to ensure demand changes are analyzed and rates adjusted accordingly.

2. Understanding the city demand via Demand calendar:

Detailed demand calendar was reviewed with all city hotel events and wedding dates . All the competing hotels events were also mapped to understand the demand for each day of the week in the upcoming 3 months. Online pricing was rationalized based on the high and low demand. Groups on high demand priced higher rates as well to yield higher rates. Room type wise selling adopted strictly to maximize the revenue during the high demand dates.

3. Utilising the STR reports to understand the trends:

From the STR 18-month trend report we identified the occupancy and ADR TRENDS of each month and planned the pricing accordingly. Weekday and weekend rate and occupancy trends were also reviewed to explore the opportunity for profitable pricing both for weekdays and weekend.

4. Utilising PERFORM to understand the forward-looking Demand:

We adopted PERFORM price recommendation with occupancy in mind to get the maximum yielding rate for the day. We also Identified the room product class wise demand for next 15 days and explored the room type wise selling to increase the ADR.

5 Targeting WHOLESALE b2b Agents:

Identified the potential B2B agents with leisure market focus supporting the competition hotels with the market intelligence. We suggest Contracts with agencies to support the weekend leisure and occupancy.

Findings through results:

We used effective pricing with the Perform and Price optimization monitoring the competition pricing for 3 months. Demand change and new contracts with commitment to room nights helped to increase the week end occupancy. This is reflected in the Segment wise performance comparison report Q2 Vs Q3.
